---
title: Policies
---

Policies decide whether an action should be **allowed**, **challenged**, or **denied**.

A policy typically evaluates a **context** object you send with each check.

Examples of context keys:

- `action`: "refund" | "deploy" | "search" | ...
- `amount`: number
- `env`: "staging" | "prod"
- `estimated_cost`: number
- `change_risk`: "low" | "high"
